About Me:

Hi there! My name is Loren Velasquez. I hold a Masters in Applied Statistics, a Bachelors in Bioinformatics and a Bachelors in Molecular Biology; all from Loyola University Chicago.

I am fascinated by data! I am especially passionate about data for social good (civic data, health data, etc.); the data with the potnetial to bring about social good & positive changes to the community.

To me, being a data enthusiast is a lot like being Indiana Jones (but with less physical danger involved, haha); The Data is the Jungle & the hidden information waiting to be uncovered are ancient artifacts just waiting to found!

I am an active member of Statistics Without Borders (SWB) and I am really able to exercise my interest for working with data as a pro bono data/statistical consultant.
